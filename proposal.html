A PhotoSharing Web App

APIs + Database Information:

  • I will be using the Unsplash API to retrieve and share photos. I also plan to integrate Instagram's API for the social media/sharing aspect.
  • The API will get various sources of information and metadata about photos, a similar photos feature, and insight to an user about photos surrounding a topic or keywords of their choice.
  • The data I will collect from users would be only username's for the sharing aspect of the web app. I will also collect data on different metrics like liked photos, number of photos shared, comments, and etc.
  • There are many data points in my database. There will be entities for a photos library for each user, each photo's metadata, comments entity, users table/entity for each user, liked photos table, authentication for users.
  • Some Examples are:
    USER -> fname, lname, dateofbirth, email, password, numPosts, comments, delPosts sharedPosts
    PHOTO -> name, type, datePosted, numLikes, numComments, author, and etc.
    PHOTO_LIB -> types, name, datePosted, categories, keywords, authors, topics, and etc.

Goals + Audience

  • The audience of my site will most likely be photographers, social media influencers, people the work in social media design, people who are passionate about photography.
  • The goals of my site are to provide a platform for people to find unique photos online and share them with like-minded individuals. Another part of that goal is giving more attention to photographers and helping build their audiences via social media. It also allows people to get much cooler pictures that are more professional than just google images.
  • I selected this topic to allow small photographer to immense skill get noticed and help them build their social media presence as well as helping others find special photos of high, professional quality for their work or entertainment.
